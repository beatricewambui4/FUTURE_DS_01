# FUTURE_DS_01 — Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Task Overview
A comprehensive analysis of business sales data covering fiscal years 2022 to 2024,
built using Power BI. The project identifies revenue trends, top-selling product 
categories, high-value customers, payment method distribution, and seasonal 
performance patterns across a B2B client base of 25 customers.

## Key Findings
- Total revenue of KSh 1,610,399.50 was generated over the 3-year period
- Butchers is the top revenue-generating category at KSh 214,300 (13.31% share)
- CUST_24 and CUST_23 are the highest-value customers by both revenue and volume
- Payment methods are evenly split: Cash 34.72%, Credit Card 32.65%, Digital Wallet 32.63%
- Furniture ranks #1 in orders but not in revenue, indicating low average order value
- Discount data contains a large NA segment (33.03%) requiring data governance attention
- 2025 data covers January only and is treated as a partial year
- Average items per order stands at 5.56 — a key upsell opportunity

## Recommendations
1. Implement a Key Account Management programme for top 5 customers
2. Develop an upselling strategy to increase average items per order beyond 5.56
3. Audit and resolve the NA discount segment across all transactions
4. Exclude 2025 from full-year comparisons until the complete dataset is available
5. Run targeted promotions on underperforming categories: Patisserie and Milk Products
6. Sort Day-of-Week chart Monday to Sunday for improved readability
